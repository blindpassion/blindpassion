About us

Blind Passion was established in 2002 and is a family business and operates from Cullen.
Our only business is the manufacture, selling and installation of window & conservatory blinds.
We manufacture Roller and Vertical Blinds and we think we produce the best quality blinds in this area and at competitive prices. We also source and sell Blinds from other reputable Brands such as Eclipse, Tropical and Arena.
